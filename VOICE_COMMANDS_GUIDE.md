## ğŸ’¡ Tips for Best Results

1. **Speak clearly** - Enunciate your words
2. **Use natural language** - Say commands naturally, don't be robotic
3. **Wait for confirmation** - Listen for audio feedback after each command
4. **Say "Hey Vision" first** - Always start with the hotword to activate listening
5. **Be specific** - "Go to settings" is better than just "settings"
6. **Combine commands** - You can say "Hey Vision, go to dashboard and refresh"
