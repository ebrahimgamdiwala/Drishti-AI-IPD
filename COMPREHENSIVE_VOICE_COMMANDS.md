## üí° Tips for Best Voice Recognition

1. **Speak Clearly** - Enunciate words clearly for better recognition
2. **Avoid Background Noise** - Reduce ambient noise for accuracy
3. **Natural Pacing** - Speak at a natural, conversational pace
4. **Short Commands** - Use the exact phrases provided for best results
5. **Proximity to Device** - Keep device near your mouth for better audio capture
6. **English Language** - Currently optimized for English speech

## ‚ùì Troubleshooting

**"I'm not sure what you meant"**: 
- The command wasn't recognized clearly
- Try rephrasing or speaking more slowly
- Check that hotword "Hey Vision" was properly detected

**No voice feedback**:
- Check that device volume is on
- Verify speech synthesis is enabled in settings
- Ensure microphone permissions are granted

**Hotword not working**:
- Make sure the app is running
- Check microphone permissions
- Try saying "Hey Vision" more clearly

---

## üîÑ Continuous Listening

The app continuously listens for the "Hey Vision" hotword and will:
- Automatically restart listening after each command
- Maintain listening in the background
- Resume listening after theme changes or other actions
- Never stop listening (unless disabled in settings)

Just speak the hotword to activate command mode!
